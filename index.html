Mediterranean Quinoa Bowl

Healthy bowl with quinoa, vegetables, and tahini dressing

Extended Preparation Time
This recipe requires more than 45 minutes to prepare. Plan accordingly!
  • 1 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3Cucumber, diced
  • 4 Red onion, sliced
  • 5 Kalamata olives
  • 6 Feta cheese, crumbled
  • 7Fresh parsley
  • 8 Tahini dressing
  • 1Rinse quinoa thoroughly. Cook according to package directions, usually 15 minutes.
  • 2While quinoa cooks, prepare all vegetables and set aside.
  • 3For tahini dressing: mix tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and water until smooth.
  • 4 Fluff cooked quinoa with a fork and let cool slightly.
  • 5 Arrange quinoa in bowls. Top with tomatoes, cucumber, onion, and olives.
  • 6 Sprinkle with feta cheese and fresh parsley. Drizzle with tahini dressing.
